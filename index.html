ReDATA is the University of Arizona's Research Data Repository

You can download datasets submitted by the UArizona community. If you are a UArizona student, staff, or faculty, you can submit your datasets for archiving to meet data retention and sharing requirements from the university and from funders or publishers. You can also download datasets licensed to the university.
